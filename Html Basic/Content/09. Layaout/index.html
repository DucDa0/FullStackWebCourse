: chỉ định nội dung độc lập, các content độc lập. Được dùng cho các: Forum post, Blog post, Newspaper Article. ex:

What Does WWF Do?

WWF's mission is to stop the degradation of our planet's natural environment, and build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature.

: chỉ định tiêu đề ex:
